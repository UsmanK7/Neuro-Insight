What is Brain Tumor?

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells in or around the brain, which can
be benign or malignant. MRI scans, enhanced by deep learning, play a crucial role in detecting tumors by identifying subtle abnormalities with high accuracy.

Treatment options include surgery, radiation, and chemotherapy, depending on the tumor's type and location.

Current methods being used in healthcare

Traditional Methods Involves Manual Segmentation OF MRI scans by a technician, followed by a doctor’s Visual Examination to identity humors, This process relies heavily on human expertise making it challenging to achieve accurate and consistent results

The Problems In Current Methods

Human error and bias can lead to inconsistent or incorrect diagnosis , which can have severe consequences for patients. moreover, Manual Segmentation is a labor process that requires significant expertise making it difficult to scale and increasing the likelihood of errors

Neuro Insight: Our Proposed Solution

Neuro Insight aims to overcome these defects by automating the segmentation and tumor detection process using machine learning algorithms. Our model can learn to accurately identity tumors and segment them into sub regions, reducing the need for manual intervention.
