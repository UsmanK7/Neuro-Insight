What is Brain Tumor?

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells in or around the brain,
which can be benign or malignant. MRI scans, enhanced by deep learning,
play a crucial role in detecting tumors by identifying subtle abnormalities with high accuracy.

Treatment options include surgery, radiation, and chemotherapy, depending on the tumor's type and location.
